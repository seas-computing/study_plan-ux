Overview

Plan of Study holds the official record of the courses accepted for each student for their concentration.
Sara Iams (Staff Co-Director of Applied Mathematics - SEAS Academic Programs) primarily uses the “Submitted Plan of Study Dashboard”.
Kathy Lovell (Undergraduate Academic Program Manager - SEAS Academic Programs) used to update “Manage Students” by adding all officially graduated students.
Margo Levine (Interim Director of Undergraduate Education - SEAS Academic Programs) manages other Plan of Study tools.

Potential areas for improvement:

  • Connect the app to my.harvard to: 
    • Auto-import student data when they log in 
    • Check course completion 
    • Work out when students have graduated 
  • For student search: 
    • Make course search and class year search work 
  • Improve page load speeds 
  • Allow download of most recent filed plan for a student 
    • Allow download of all plans?

Aims of the project:

  • Ensure the accessibility of the tool 
  • Make it easier for your students to plan their concentrations 
  • Enable you to review the study plans more easily, and manage the concentration 
  • Enable other concentrations to use the tool 
  • Build an application that we can support more easily

Current interface - user flows

User flow is the path taken by a prototypical user on a website or app to complete a task. The user flow takes them from their entry point through a set of steps towards a successful outcome and final action, such as purchasing a product.

Approve/deny study plan

Using the interface "View plan of study" Sara approves or denies plans of study submitted by students.

Since the application is very slow, she opens plans of study in separate browser tabs.

Manage students

Kathy used to maintain the information in "Manage Students".

To review a study plan of an inactive or graduated student, student's status has to be temporarily changed to "active".

my.Harvard

Sara uses my.harvard to verify information about students' academic term courses.

my.harvard desired functionality

  • All currently and active, inactive and graduated students per concentration (from my.harvard)
    • Although in my.harvard it may take a long time to update student status (from active to inactive and vice versa)
    • Graduated status is more reliable on my.harvard
    • My.harvard also indicates which courses a student has completed
  • Download spreadsheet with Study Plan information (a spreadsheet can already be downloaded from my.harvard)
    • The most recent plan filed for each student
    • Auditing and coordination with my.harvard
    • Also all plans with “Denied” and “Filed” status
  • Sync above spreadsheets to updated Study Plan records

my.harvard pros

  • Graduated status is more reliable on my.harvard
  • my.harvard also indicates which courses a student has completed

my.harvard cons

  • It may take a long time to update student status (from active to inactive and vice versa)
  • Text entry fields with little checking
  • “Cocktail” courses entry, not accepted in a concentration
  • Useless student management tools (hence Study Plan)

Current interface - screen captures

Screen captures of current administrative interface, dated December 2020.

Administrator Panel

This interface accesses Plan of Study tools.

View plan of study

This interface is access via the "Submitted Plan of Study Dashboard" link on the Administrative Panel. It allows review, notation, approval or denial of study plans submitted by students.

Although useful, the "Auto-check" feature has been inactivated.
Desired Functionality - Sections of "Course Selection" should be automatically assigned.

Student search and filtering

"Search & Filter" has limited functionality. Not all filters (input forms) work as they should.

Status of student submitted plans

Search result list shows filtered students, color coded by status.
The expanded view of a student (see screen capture below) shows all the submitted plans and their review status from both Auto-check (Fail, Pass) and the reviewer (Denied, Approved).

Manage Students

Student status is reviewed and edited in this interface.
Student status is updated upon change of concentration, indefinite leave, or to review a study plan of student who is no longer active.
